Ken Griffey Jr.

George Kenneth Griffey Jr. nicknamed "Junior" and "the Kid"

Ken Griffey Jr baseball career.

  • Teams
  • Seattle Mariners (1989-1999)
  • Cincinnati Reds (200-2008)
  • Chicago White Sox (2008)
  • Seattle Mariners (2009-2010)
  • Career highlights and awards
  • 13 time All-Star (1990-2000, 2004, 2007)
  • AL MVP (1997)
  • 10 time Gold Glove Award (1990-1999)
  • 7 time Silver Slugger Award (1991, 1993, 1994, 1996-1999)
  • NL Comeback Player of the Year (2005)
  • 4 time AL home run leader (1994, 1997-1999
  • AL RBI leader (1997)
  • Seattle Mariners No. 24 retired
  • Seattle Mariners Hall of Fame
  • Cincinnati Reds Hall of Fame
  • Major League Baseball All-Century Team
  • Baseball Hall of Fame (2016) (99.32% first ballot votes)
